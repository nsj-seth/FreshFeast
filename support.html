Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time by visiting the Order Tracking page and entering your order number. You'll also receive SMS updates.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery fee is $3.99 for all orders. Free delivery on orders above $30!

Can I modify my order?

Yes, you can modify your order within 5 minutes of placing it. Contact support immediately for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery.

How long does delivery take?

Average delivery time is 30-45 minutes depending on your location and order complexity.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes! We have a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan options. Use the menu filters to find them.
